High byte ^GGcVi7Vu

0x20 0x21 0x22 0x23 ^P3HwM2nG

ENDIANNESS ^OiM93RPh

In the Big Endian method, the bytes of the data are stored from the low order to the
high order ^9JfUASAj

Each cell is one byte ^XFqLWsvS

In the Little Endian method, the bytes of data are stored from the higher order bytes to
the lower order bytes ^lCTkDDUB
